Upload Your Founder Video

Upload a 5–20 minute MP4 video. Social Spark Studio will transcribe it, identify founder-aligned moments, and prepare campaign-ready posts.

MP4 video is the main path. Audio files (MP3/M4A/WAV) work as a backup.

Or import from an RSS feed

Have a recurring show? Paste the RSS feed from your podcast/audio host and choose a 5–20 minute audio episode to transcribe.

Where do I find my RSS feed?

  • Go to the platform where your show/audio episodes are published (your podcast or audio host).
  • Look for a link called RSS Feed, Podcast RSS, or Copy RSS Link and paste the URL here.
  • YouTube links and Channel IDs are not supported — YouTube blocks feed access from cloud servers. Upload your MP4 video above instead.
