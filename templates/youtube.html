Choose an audio episode from your RSS feed. Social Spark Studio is designed for 5–20 minute founder videos, lives, or podcast-style episodes.

Best for lives, recurring shows, and YouTube channel content. Paste your RSS feed, choose an audio episode, and Social Spark Studio will transcribe it.

Where do I find my RSS feed?

  • Go to the platform where your show/audio episodes are published.
  • Look for a link called RSS Feed, Podcast RSS, Distribution Feed, or Copy RSS Link.
  • Paste the RSS feed URL here.
  • A YouTube video URL is not the same as an RSS feed.
